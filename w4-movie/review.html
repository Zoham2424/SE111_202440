Review

Ian Alvarado August 8, 2024

The movie I am legend is a post-apocalyptic movie, following the story of Dr.Robert Neville. In the movie we are able to see Robert surviving in an empty city during the day, at night hordes of zombies will lurk around. In I am legend Robert is looking for the cure for the virus. He has a companion Dog "Sam" that helps him travel the city looking for other humans, we can see him become desperate for human connection to the point of alucinations. The turn point comes when Robert makes a mistake he does not comeback home before dark. He is save by Anna that is traveling with his son Ethan looking for a safe place.

My favorite actor by far is the dog, who was played by Abbey and Kona 2 amazing German Shepperd. Everytime I see the movie I wish a dog like Sam

Trivia

  • Will Smith grew so enamored of his canine co-star, Abbey, that he tried to adopt her when filming was finished, but the dog's trainer could not be persuaded to give her up.
  • When Dr. Robert Neville sees the mannequin he calls "Fred" outside on the street, it looks like the mannequin moves its head a little. During that scene, a mime replaced the mannequin to try and play with people's minds when they watched that scene.
  • The trainer put a piece of tape on Abbey's paw to make her limp, after Sam gets injured fighting the infected dogs.
